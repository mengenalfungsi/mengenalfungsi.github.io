10. CONTOH EXPRESSIONS OF ATTITUDE


Adam : Hey, how are you today?

Hubert : I'm fine. How about you?

Adam : I'm fine too. Did you watch the ads on tv recently?

Hubert : Yeah .... that ads is so funny.

Adam : I agree with you. That's we called it funny ads technique, because that ads makes people laugh.

Hubert : I don't think so. It's quite possible that technique is emotional appeal.

Adam : As far as i'm concerned, Emotional Appeal technique is ads technique that try to relate to your emotional level. For instance, i saw the soap brand ads said that if you didn't use this soap, you would get killed by the bactery.

Hubert : Oh, i'm so scared.

Adam : Don't be scared, that's we called it emotional appeal techniques. It try to relate to us on an emotional level.

Hubert : Oh i see. Now i know what is the different between funny ads technique and emotional appeal technique. Thank's my friend.

Adam : No problem.

Keterangan dari dialog mengenai expressing attitude dan annoyance

1. I agree with you : termasuk dalam kata expressing agreement
2. because : termasuk dalam kata expressing justification
3. I don't think so : termasuk dalam kata expressing disagreement
4. It's quite possible that : termasuk dalam kata expressing speculation
5. As far as i'm concerned : termasuk dalam kata expressing thinking expressions
6. i'm so scared : termasuk dalam kata expressing annoyance
7. Don't be scared : termasuk dalam kata reducing someone's annoyance

12. Pak Wayan adalah seorang pemilik vila di Bali. Ia ingin mengetahui pengaruh peningkatan harga pada vila miliknya terhadap banyak customer. Jika Pak Wayan memasang harga Rp900.000,00 per malam per vila, rata-rata customer yang menyewa vila setiap minggu ada 20 orang. Untuk setiap pembelian sebesar Rp100.000,00, rata-rata customer setiap minggu berkurang 2 orang. Misalkan x adalah kenaikan harga vila, p(x) merupakan fungsi pendapatan per vila, dan q(x) merupakan fungsi yang menyatakan rata-rata banyak customer setiap minggu. Tentukan fungsi p(x)!​


Fungsi p(x) dapat ditemukan dengan mengalikan harga per malam per vila (Rp900.000,00) dengan rata-rata banyak customer per minggu (20 orang), kemudian ditambahkan dengan kenaikan harga vila (x) dikali dengan rata-rata penurunan customer per pembelian (2 orang per Rp100.000,00).

Maka, fungsi p(x) dapat dituliskan sebagai berikut:
p(x) = (20 - (2/100000)*(x))*900000

Jadi, fungsi p(x) adalah p(x) = (20 - (2/100000)*(x))*900000.
