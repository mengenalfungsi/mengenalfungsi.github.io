(+) The earth revolves around the Sun

(+) S + V1

The man’s data (steal) more than once. 3. Listening to the music (be) always her hobby. 4. Ayu(must; submit) her paper by email. 5. You (not can; break)the glass by just looking at it. 6. He (examine) by the internist on the second floor yesterday. 7. The earth (revolve) on its axis once every twenty four hours. 8. The delivery charge (inform) tomorrow. 9. People’s attitudes (change)by some soap operas. 10. Digital TV (invent) in the 1960s.


Change the verb in parentheses to fit into the tenses and the voice.

Answers

1. Until now, some friends have broadcast the event three times.  

2. The man’s data have been stolen more than once.  

3. Listening to the music is always her hobby.  

4. Ayu must submit her paper by email.  

5. You can not break the glass by just looking at it.  

6. He was examined by the internist on the second floor yesterday.  

7. The Earth revolves on its axis once every twenty four hours.  

8. The delivery charge will be informed tomorrow.  

9. People’s attitudes are changed by some soap operas.  

10. Digital TV was invented in the 1960s.

---------------

Explanation

1. Ada keterangan until now dan three times. Gunakan present perfect (S + have/has + v₃)

2. Ada jumlah bebrapa kali more than once. Gunakan present perfect.

3. Ada keterangan frekuensi always. Gunakan present simple. Kata kerja "be" dalam present simple dikonjugasi menjadi am/is/are.

4 dan 5 Sesudah modal verb (misalnya can dan must), gunakan bare infinitive (v₁).

6. Kalimatnya pasif (S + be + v₃) karena ada frasa by the internist (oleh dokter penyakit dalam). Karena keterangan waktunya yesterday, berarti tense yang digunakan adalah past simple. "be" dalam past adalah was/were.

7. Ada adverb of frequency once every twenty four hours yang menunjukkan suatu habit dan pernyataan ini merupakan general truth maka gunakan present simple (S + v₁/v-s). Karena subjeknya tunggal gunakan v-s.

8. Kalimatnya pasif karena ongkos kirim diinformasikan, bukan yang menginformasikan. Keterangan waktu tomorrow (future tense), gunakan modal will atau am/is/are going to.

Alternatif jawaban: The delivery charge is going to be informed tomorrow.

9. Kalimat pasif karena ada by .... Tidak ada keterangan waktu, gunakan saja present simple. Lihat no 2 dan 6.

10. Kalimat pasif karena TV diciptakan, bukan menciptakan. Past tense karena tahun 1960an lampau.
