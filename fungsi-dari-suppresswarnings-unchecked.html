CS: Yes Of course, Sir! Do you see three dots at the upside corner on the right side of your browser, Sir?

Me: Yes, what about it?

CS: Please click it and find setting there, Sir!

Me: Okay, I found it! What's next?

CS: Good, next, click Autofill and you can see many boxes there, Sir!

Me: Yes, next please?

CS: Find the Automatically load image box and unchecked it, Sir!

Me: Done!

CS: Great, now you can reboot your computer and try to connect to the internet again, Sir!
