download lah di play store

Cara download Get Rich Jepang :

1. Langkah pertama, download dan Intall 1Mobile market terlebih dahulu di sini

2. Setelah proses install selesai cari aplikasinya dengan keyword "line get rich" dikolom search

3. Pilih yang kedua atau yang bergambar Diana yang memegang dadu putih/basic atau lebih jelasnya seperti gambar dibawah.

4. Lalu klik Free/install. Nah pada gambar dibawah tertulis "open" karena saya sudah memiliki aplikasinya.

5. Tunggu proses pengambilan datanya.

6. Jeng-jeng anda sudah bisa memainkan Get rich server Jepang

Cara download Get Rich Taiwan :

1. Langkah pertama, download dan Intall 1Mobile market terlebih dahulu di sini

2. Setelah proses install selesai cari aplikasinya dengan keyword "LINE 旅遊大亨" dikolom search. (silahkan copy keyword tersebut)

3. Pilih yang kedua atau yang bergambar Dennis yang memegang dadu putih/basic atau lebih jelasnya seperti gambar dibawah.

4. Lalu klik Free/install untuk menginstal.

5. Tunggu proses pengambilan datanya.

6. Jeng-jeng anda sudah bisa memainkan Get rich server Taiwan

Cara download Get Rich Korea :

1. Download Kakao Talk di Playstore ( Wajib, Karena log in menggunakan Kakao Talk bukan Line )

2. Download dan Intall 1Mobile market terlebih dahulu di sini

3. Setelah proses install selesai cari aplikasinya dengan keyword "Modoo For Kakao" dikolom search

20. Kinds off greeting card 1.birthday card is 2.Thank you card is 3. Get well card is 4. Congratulasion card is 5. Holiday card is 6. Condolence card is​


Jawaban:

1. Birthday card is a greeting card given or sent to a person to celebrate their birthday

2. Thank you card is a card to show your appreciation for a gift or service that someone has provided for you.

3. Get well card is sent to a person who is unwell, expressing a wish for a speedy recovery

4. Congratulation card is giving someone a special card with our good wishes for them and for a happy event

5. Holiday card is a reflection of your sentiment during the holiday season and often make family and friends feel better

6. Condolence card is a way to express your sympathy through cards or letters is one way to provide comfort and peace of mind to someone who is mourning
